ENIGMA

The very first initiative of its kind, thrives on catapulting efforts of highly driven individuals towards realizing projects that will not only prime them for universities abroad and set them up for their professional journey but make them an essential part of making ICAS a more connected community than it has ever been.

WHAT ARE WE DOING HERE?

Working on valuable projects:
Our club has a strong focus on project based learning, something that you will be subjected to in your further journey in this field. Projects will be focussed on learning basic concepts first and then solving real world problems.

OUR GOAL

A novel way of savouring your days at college!

Any kind of platform, that brings together people in collaborating to achieve common objectives, and landmarks of learning that coincide with their personal interests is always a delight for any student. On getting the glimpse of the sheer variety of interests our club aims to cater to through its objectives, one will come across our wholistic approach towards learning, which is to realise how fun it can be, by observing what parts of the world it influences.
